Roasted Cauliflower Salad

This hearty cauliflower salad could double as a holiday side dish and as an everyday veggie meal. It's packed with contrasting textures and flavours, and it really is energising and bright.

Ingredients

  • 1 cauliflower
  • 2 cups arugula
  • Lemon wedges
  • 1/2 cup Tahini Sauce
  • 1/4 cup Pickled Onions
  • nuts
  • dried apricots
  • chopped olives
  • Microgreens

Instructions

  1. Roast Cauliflower
  2. In a medium bowl, toss the arugula and roasted cauliflower with a drizzle of olive oil, a squeeze of lemon
